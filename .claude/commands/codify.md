Document a learning, decision, or discovery.

Usage: /codify <what was learned>

Learning: $ARGUMENTS

Steps:

1. Determine where this belongs:
   - Experiment results → `LEARNINGS.md`
   - Architecture/design → `docs/architecture.md`
   - Development workflow → `docs/development_process.md`
   - Research findings → `docs/research_landscape.md` or `readinglist.md`
   - Agent/project rules → `CLAUDE.md`
   - Future investigation → `docs/future-work.md`

2. Write the learning clearly:
   - What was discovered
   - Why it matters
   - How it affects future work

3. Keep it concise.

Every undocumented lesson will be repeated by the next agent.
